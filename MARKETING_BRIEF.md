# Skrolz — Marketing Website Brief

**Document purpose:** Brief for the marketing website developer.  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 2026  

---

## 1. Product Overview

**Skrolz** is a vertical, full-screen micro-reading app in the style of TikTok, but for short written content: one story at a time. Users swipe through bite-sized posts and stories in a focused, distraction-free feed.

**Tagline:** *Short reads. One story at a time.*

**One-liner:** Swipe. Read. Go. Skrolz delivers bite-sized content in a TikTok-style feed—short posts and stories, one full-screen at a time.

---

## 2. Target Audience

- **Primary:** Readers who want short, scrollable content without long articles or video.
- **Secondary:** Creators and curators who want to share short posts and multi-slide stories.
- **Geographic focus:** Arabic + English markets (app is localized for both).
- **Age:** Broad, skewing toward 18–35.
- **Behavior:** Short attention spans, mobile-first, values “snackable” content and visual clarity.

---

## 3. Core Value Propositions

| Value | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Bite-sized content** | Micro-content (≤280 char posts, multi-slide stories) instead of long reads. |
| **One story at a time** | Full-screen, vertical feed—no endless scroll, no clutter. |
| **AI-powered curation** | Stories picked for you and transparent “why you saw this” explanations. |
| **Save & revisit** | Bookmarks and collections so you can revisit content at your own pace. |
| **Create in minutes** | Write posts, create lessons, or use AI to draft posts. |

---

## 4. Key Features (For Marketing Copy)

### For Readers
- **For You feed** — Personalized stories based on interests and behavior.
- **Following** — Content from people and collections you follow.
- **Trending** — What’s popular across the app.
- **Curated** — Hand-picked playlists and collections.
- **Bookmarks** — Save posts and lessons for later.
- **Focus mode** — Distraction-free reading.
- **Context & sources** — See citations and why content was shown.

### For Creators
- **Write Post** — Short posts (max 280 characters, like tweets).
- **AI Post** — Generate multiple post variants from a topic.
- **Create Lesson** — Multi-slide lessons with quizzes.
- **Study Buddy (Premium)** — AI study tips, action items, and micro-quizzes for any topic or lesson.
- **Collections** — Curated playlists of posts and lessons.
- **Creator stats** — Likes, saves, comments, and daily performance.

### Platform & UX
- **Bilingual** — English and Arabic (RTL).
- **Offline support** — Cached feed for offline reading.
- **Push notifications** — Stay updated on new content and engagement.
- **Accessibility** — Settings for readability and usability.

---

## 5. Premium / Monetization

- **Premium subscription** via RevenueCat (in-app purchases).
- Premium features include: Study Buddy, possibly advanced AI tools, and additional curation.
- Free tier: core feed, bookmarks, following, and basic creation.

## 6. Brand & Visual Identity

### Design Direction
- **Style:** Modern, dark-first with glassmorphism and purple accents.
- **Year:** Feels like a 2026 product—translucent surfaces, gradients, and clear typography.

### Color Palette
| Role | Hex | Usage |
|------|-----|--------|
| Primary | `#8B5CF6` | Main accent (deep purple/violet) |
| Primary dark | `#6B4FFC` | Alternate purple |
| Accent | `#EC4899` | Highlights (pink-purple) |
| Accent secondary | `#A855F7` | Secondary accent |
| Dark background | `#0A0A0F` | Main dark background |
| Dark surface | `#0F0F14` | Secondary dark |
| Light background | `#FAFAFA` | Light mode |
| Success | `#10B981` | Positive actions |
| Danger | `#EF4444` | Errors, destructive actions |

### Typography
- **English:** Plus Jakarta Sans (Google Fonts)
- **Arabic:** Cairo (Google Fonts)

### Gradients (for hero, CTAs, premium)
- **Primary:** `#8B5CF6` → `#EC4899`
- **Accent:** `#6B4FFC` → `#EC4899`
- **Premium:** `#EC4899` → `#A855F7`
- **Dark background:** `#0A0A0F` → `#0F0F14`

- **Voice:** Confident but approachable. “Short reads. Full screen. Zero scroll.”
- **Key phrases:** “One story at a time”, “Bite-sized content”, “Swipe. Read. Go.”, “Stories picked for you.”

---

## 8. Competitive Context

- **TikTok/Reels:** Short-form vertical video. Skrolz is vertical and full-screen but text-first.
- **Twitter/X:** Short text posts. Skrolz adds stories, collections, and full-screen reading.
- **Medium/Substack:** Long-form. Skrolz is short reads, creator-driven, and feed-based.
- **Differentiator:** Vertical, full-screen micro-reading + stories + AI curation in one app.
